WHO I AM

Hi there! I am Marian, a developer in training. I am an always curious and open minded human being. I like most things related to tech (but hey, that's what all programmers say, right?). I have a keen eye when it comes to design, but when I get a problem solved, the feeling of joy is amazing. I'm generally a stubborn person when it comes to my work, liking things done the right way. You will almost never hear me saying, "Hey, that works. It's good enough, leave it that way".

I started my web development journey about 4 months ago. Working and learning on open source platforms like The Odin Project and freeCodeCamp. It's been a lot of fun. Recently I dipped my toe in the "JavaScript Ocean", once I got a fair shot at HTML and CSS. It's intimidating, but never enough to make me quit.
